History of Computers - Abridged

1946

ENIAC created, first electronic computer. Project Whirlwind begins which leads to the perfection of magnetic core memory.

1972

C, based on BPCL, is released. Pong is released, launching the era of video games.

1981

IBM releases IBM 5150, setting the standards for future commercial personal computers. MS-DOS released.

1989

Deep Thought defeats chess master David Levy. Nintendo GameBoy releases alongside Tetris.

1994

Sony releases the PlayStation, completely changing home entertainment systems. ESRB founded. CompactFlash memory cards released.
